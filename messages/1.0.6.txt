## v1.1.0 update message

now support and only support
	1. HTML(hex style and dec style, but NOT entitles style)
		e.g. '&#x4e2d;&#x534e;' <=> '中华'
			 '&#20013;&#33775;' <=> '中華'
	2. CSS(font-family property)
		e.g. '\5b8b\4f53' <=> '宋体'
	3. JS(hex style and unicode style)
		e.g. '\u6c49\u5b57' <=> '汉字'
		      '\u{0001d306}' <=> '𝌆'
